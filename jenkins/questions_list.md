# Jenkins Interview Questions

## Theory Questions
1. What is Jenkins, and what is the difference between declarative and scripted pipelines?
2. Explain the Jenkins master-agent (controller-agent) architecture.
3. How do you manage plugins and their dependencies in Jenkins?
4. What are Jenkins shared libraries, and why would you use them?
5. How can you trigger a Jenkins pipeline automatically when code is pushed to a repository?

## Practical Scenarios
6. **Scenario:** A Jenkins build agent is repeatedly going offline. How do you troubleshoot this?
7. **Scenario:** Your Jenkins pipeline failed at the "Deploy" stage. How do you resume the build from that stage instead of starting over?
8. **Scenario:** You need to securely use an SSH key in a Jenkins pipeline to connect to a remote server. How do you handle the credentials?
9. **Scenario:** A specific pipeline is consuming all available executors on the controller, blocking other jobs. How do you restrict its execution or optimize it?
10. **Scenario:** Your Jenkins controller's disk is full. What are the common culprits and how do you fix it without losing data?
